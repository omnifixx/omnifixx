What problem does our business idea solve or what needs does it satisfy?

Our business idea, which aims to provide maid, cook, babysitter and similar domestic help placement services, aims to solve several problems and needs:

  • Time saving - (limited time and resources to find suitable domestic help or service providers)
  • Reliability and safety - (Our business idea facilitates the search for trustworthy service providers by enabling thorough checks and evaluations)
  • Need for skilled labor - (Our business idea creates job opportunities for domestic workers and service providers looking for opportunities to offer their services)
  • Flexibility - (Our business idea allows users to book services on demand)

What is Omnifixx?

Our business idea is to provide services for the placement of domestic workers and similar household help. In today's fast-paced world, many individuals and families find themselves juggling multiple responsibilities, leaving them with limited time and energy to dedicate to household chores and caregiving duties.

By offering a platform or agency that specializes in matching qualified domestic workers with families or individuals in need of assistance, you're not only addressing a practical need but also facilitating mutually beneficial relationships. Families can find reliable and trustworthy help to manage their homes, while domestic workers can secure employment opportunities that match their skills and preferences.
